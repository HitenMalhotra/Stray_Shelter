A Plan for Every Stray's Needs

We are promoting following principles:

PREVENTION

  • Education and awareness.
  • Spay/neuter programs.
  • Promote shelter adoption.
  • Report abuse/neglect.
  • Stronger animal laws.

RESCUE

  • Promote adoption.
  • Support rescue organizations.
  • Volunteer at shelters.
  • Foster animals in need.
  • Provide financial aid.

PROTECTION

  • Advocate for rights.
  • End animal cruelty.
  • Combat illegal trade.
  • Preserve habitats.
  • Support conservation.
